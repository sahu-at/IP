About Ganpat University

Ganpat University is promoted by Ganpat Vidyanagar, a trust that aims to provide quality higher education with strong industry orientation. The campus is located at Kherva, Mehsana-Gozaria highway, spread across hundreds of acres with modern infrastructure.

The University offers diploma, undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in diverse academic disciplines such as Engineering & Technology, Management, Computer Applications, Pharmacy, Sciences, Agriculture, Maritime Studies, Design, and more.

Teaching-learning is supported by advanced laboratories, industry-linked projects, research centers and active collaboration with national and international organizations.

Vision

To be a globally recognized university known for innovation, research, and value-based education that nurtures socially responsible professionals.

Mission

To provide outcome-based education, establish industry–academia partnerships, encourage entrepreneurship, and support all-round development of students.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Inclusiveness, Innovation and Social Commitment.

Approvals & Recognition

UGC recognized; programs approved by relevant councils (like AICTE, PCI, etc.) as applicable. Please verify latest status on the official website.

Step-by-step Admission Process

1

Check Eligibility: Choose your program (Diploma / UG / PG / Ph.D) and check minimum qualifying marks, required subjects and entrance exam criteria (if any).

2

Online Application: Fill the application form on the official admission portal, upload documents and pay the application fee.

3

Merit / Entrance / Counseling: Depending on the program, admission may be based on merit, entrance scores (like GUJCET/other) or personal interview/counseling.

4

Offer & Fee Payment: Selected candidates receive provisional admission offer and are required to confirm by paying fees within the given timeline.

5

Document Verification & Registration: Submit original documents for verification at campus or designated center, complete registration and join the orientation/induction program.

Campus Life & Facilities

Ganpat University is known as Green Campus. It has many facilities , they are :

Hostel & Dining

Separate hostels for boys and girls with adequate capacity, mess facilities, Wi-Fi and basic amenities for comfortable stay.

Sports & Recreation

Grounds for cricket, football, athletics, indoor stadium, gymnasium and regular sports events to promote physical fitness.

Laboratories & Libraries

Well-equipped labs in engineering, pharmacy, sciences, computing and a central library with digital resources and reading halls.

Clubs & Activities

Technical, cultural, social and innovation clubs organize regular events, competitions, seminars and fests for holistic development.
